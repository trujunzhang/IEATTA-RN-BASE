Hear ye, hear ye, all milk tea lovers across the land! Want to be pampered and spoiled on a wonderful Monday evening like the Duke and Duchess of Bobashire? RSVP to this local sampling gig by Tea Lyfe for a relaxing, enjoyable evening of tea tasting and light bites with like-minded royalties ;)

What can you expect?
+ A delightfully royal night of tea tasting
+ Light bites and eats
+ Relaxing music and fun ice breaker
+ Free SWAG SWAG SWAG. Come early; don't miss out on it!
This is a FREE sampling event but tips for our host and vendors are greatly appreciated.

About the Host, Tea Lyfe Drinks:
Our mission to provide drinks made with the best premium ingredients. Our selection of drinks are produced with high grade loose leaves and organic blends from well selected sources. We pride ourselves in using Northern California milk that comes from green pastured cows as well as local raw honey. We wanted to open a shop that sells only the best organic ingredients for handcrafted specialty drinks. The value and health benefits of tea is one of the reasons we want our customers to try out our natural infused drinks and know that everything going into your body is pure. So get ready for some tea in your lyfe!
